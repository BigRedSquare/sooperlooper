Donate

If you enjoy SooperLooper and you find yourself using the software regularly please consider making a monetary donation. If you use it professionally, consider a somewhat larger donation. Along with the personal satisfaction you might feel from supporting free software, feature requests from donators are likely to receive a higher priority for implementation.

Any donation amount will be welcomed, but we kindly suggest a US $30 donation for regular use, and a US $50 for professional use. Donations are handled through PayPal. You don't need a PayPal account to donate, secure credit card transactions are supported.
